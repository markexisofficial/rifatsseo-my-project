Client FAQ

Common Questions from Clients

How long until I see results?

Most clients see initial improvements within 30-60 days, with significant results typically visible within 3-4 months. Local SEO and GMB optimization often show faster results than traditional organic SEO. Technical fixes can show immediate improvements in site performance.

What's your communication style?

I believe in transparent, regular communication. You'll receive detailed monthly reports, weekly progress updates, and I'm always available via email or scheduled calls to discuss strategy, results, and next steps. No SEO jargon - just clear explanations.

How do you measure success?

Success is measured through KPIs that matter to your business: keyword rankings, local pack visibility, organic traffic growth, GBP impressions and actions, lead generation, and ultimately ROI. Every campaign has clear, measurable goals established upfront.

Do you require long-term contracts?

While SEO is a long-term strategy, I work on flexible terms. Initial projects typically run 3-6 months to achieve meaningful results. Many clients choose to continue on a monthly retainer for ongoing optimization, but there's no pressure or lock-in contracts.
