Case Study #01
Local SEO • GMB Optimization

Tree Service Company Achieves 4x Lead Growth in Competitive Market

Client Background

A mid-sized tree service company serving multiple cities in a competitive metropolitan area. Despite offering quality services, they struggled to compete with established competitors in local search results and were generating minimal leads from online searches.

Industry: Tree Services / Arborist
Service Area: 5 Cities (15-mile radius)
Duration: 6 Months
Focus: Local SEO, GMB Optimization, GEO Strategy

The Problem

  • Google Business Profile was incomplete and unoptimized (missing categories, poor description)
  • Only ranking on page 3-4 for primary service keywords like "tree removal [city]"
  • Zero visibility in the Local 3-Pack for any target keywords
  • Inconsistent NAP (Name, Address, Phone) across web directories
  • Website had 15+ technical issues affecting mobile performance
  • No geo-targeted content or service area pages
  • Only 8 Google reviews (average 3.9 stars)
  • Generating only 2-3 qualified leads per month from organic search

SEO Strategy Implemented

Phase 1: GBP Deep Optimization (Month 1)

Complete overhaul of Google Business Profile: optimized all categories, wrote keyword-rich business description, added service menu with 12 specific services, uploaded 25 high-quality geo-tagged images, set up service areas for all 5 cities, and created weekly Google Posts.

Phase 2: Citation Building & NAP Consistency (Month 1-2)

Built 45 high-authority local citations across platforms including Yelp, Yellow Pages, BBB, Angie's List, HomeAdvisor, and industry-specific directories. Ensured 100% NAP consistency across all platforms.

Phase 3: Technical SEO Fixes (Month 2)

Resolved all 15 technical issues including broken links, slow-loading images, mobile responsiveness problems, missing meta tags, and implemented Local Business Schema markup on all pages.

Phase 4: Geo-Targeted Content Development (Month 2-3)

Created dedicated service area pages for each of the 5 cities with geo-specific content, local landmarks, and service coverage details. Implemented silo structure and strategic internal linking.

Phase 5: Review Generation & Reputation Management (Month 3-6)

Implemented systematic review request strategy after each completed job. Provided response templates for all reviews (positive and negative). Grew from 8 reviews to 47 reviews with 4.8-star average.

Phase 6: Link Building & Authority Growth (Month 3-6)

Strategic backlink campaign targeting local business associations, sponsored community events, local news websites, and relevant industry blogs. Secured 18 high-quality local backlinks.

Case Study #02
Technical SEO • On-Page Optimization

Roofing Company Recovers From Google Penalty and Achieves 3x Traffic Growth

Client Background

An established roofing company with 15+ years of experience faced a severe drop in rankings after a Google algorithm update. Their website traffic plummeted by 60%, and they were struggling to generate leads despite having a strong reputation in the local market.

Industry: Roofing Services
Service Area: 8 Cities (25-mile radius)
Duration: 5 Months
Focus: Technical SEO, Penalty Recovery, On-Page Optimization

The Problem

  • Website traffic dropped 60% after Google Core Update
  • 52 critical technical errors identified in comprehensive audit
  • PageSpeed score of only 38 on mobile (extremely slow loading)
  • Thin content on service pages (200-300 words only)
  • No proper heading structure or keyword optimization
  • Duplicate meta descriptions across 70% of pages
  • Poor mobile experience with broken layouts on key pages
  • Toxic backlinks from spammy directories affecting domain authority

SEO Strategy Implemented

Phase 1: Comprehensive Technical Audit (Month 1)

Conducted deep-dive audit using Screaming Frog, SEMrush, and Google Search Console. Identified 52 critical issues including broken internal links, missing canonical tags, slow-loading resources, poor URL structure, and indexation problems.

Phase 2: Technical Issue Resolution (Month 1-2)

Systematically resolved all 52 technical issues: fixed broken links, implemented proper 301 redirects, optimized images (reduced size by 65%), enabled browser caching, minified CSS/JS files, and implemented lazy loading for images.

Phase 3: Core Web Vitals Optimization (Month 2)

Focused optimization on Core Web Vitals: improved Largest Contentful Paint (LCP) from 5.2s to 1.8s, First Input Delay (FID) from 180ms to 45ms, and Cumulative Layout Shift (CLS) from 0.28 to 0.05. Achieved 91 PageSpeed score on mobile.

Phase 4: On-Page Content Optimization (Month 2-3)

Rewrote all 15 service pages with comprehensive content (800-1,200 words), proper keyword optimization, semantic keyword variations, strategic header tag structure (H1-H4), and conversion-focused CTAs.

Phase 5: Schema Markup Implementation (Month 3)

Implemented comprehensive schema markup: LocalBusiness schema, Service schema for each roofing service, Review schema to display star ratings, and FAQ schema on key landing pages.

Phase 6: Toxic Link Cleanup & Quality Link Building (Month 3-5)

Disavowed 87 toxic backlinks through Google Disavow Tool. Launched strategic link building campaign earning 22 high-quality backlinks from local news sites, industry associations, and relevant business directories.

Case Study #03
Answer Engine Optimization • Content Strategy

Lawn Care Business Captures 15 Featured Snippets and Doubles Organic Traffic

Client Background

A growing lawn care and landscaping business looking to differentiate from competitors through content marketing and educational resources. They wanted to be seen as the trusted expert in their market while capturing voice search traffic and featured snippets.

Industry: Lawn Care & Landscaping
Service Area: 3 Counties (20-mile radius)
Duration: 4 Months
Focus: AEO, Content Strategy, Featured Snippets

The Problem

  • Minimal organic traffic from informational searches (only transactional)
  • Website lacked educational content or helpful resources
  • Zero featured snippets captured across all keywords
  • Missing opportunities for voice search optimization
  • No FAQ pages or question-based content
  • Competitors dominated "how to" and "best time to" searches
  • Content thin on actual service pages (300-400 words)
  • No schema markup for FAQ or How-To content

SEO Strategy Implemented

Phase 1: Question-Based Keyword Research (Month 1)

Identified 120+ question-based keywords using Answer the Public, SEMrush, and Google's "People Also Ask" feature. Focused on "how to," "when to," "best time," and "what is" queries related to lawn care.

Phase 2: Content Strategy & Topic Clustering (Month 1)

Developed comprehensive content strategy with topic clusters around main service pillars: lawn maintenance, fertilization, pest control, landscaping, and seasonal care. Created content calendar prioritizing featured snippet opportunities.

Phase 3: FAQ & How-To Content Creation (Month 1-3)

Created 18 comprehensive FAQ pages and 12 detailed How-To guides (1,200-1,800 words each) optimized specifically for featured snippet capture. Each piece structured with clear questions, concise answers, and detailed explanations.

Phase 4: AEO Schema Implementation (Month 2-3)

Implemented FAQ Schema on all FAQ pages, How-To Schema on guide content, and enhanced service pages with detailed Q&A sections using proper schema markup. All content optimized for voice search with natural language.

Phase 5: Internal Linking & Content Clusters (Month 3)

Built strong internal linking architecture connecting pillar pages to supporting content. Created logical silos ensuring proper topic authority distribution and optimal link equity flow.

Phase 6: Snippet Optimization & Monitoring (Month 3-4)

Continuously monitored snippet opportunities, refined content formatting (tables, lists, step-by-step instructions), and optimized answer lengths (40-60 words for paragraph snippets).
