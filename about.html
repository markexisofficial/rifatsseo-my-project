Hi, I'm Rakibur Rahman

SEO Specialist | Focus: Geo-Targeted & Answer Engine Optimization (AEO)

I'm a results-driven SEO Specialist with over 3 years of hands-on experience specializing in high-return Geo-targeted (GEO) campaigns and advanced Answer Engine Optimization (AEO). My expertise lies in leveraging Google Business Profile (GBP), structured data (Schema), and local citations to help businesses dominate competitive local markets and capture high-intent voice-search traffic.

Throughout my career, I have successfully diagnosed and resolved over 120 critical technical SEO issues across a portfolio of 45+ diverse projects, consistently delivering significant measurable improvements in local rankings, map visibility, and the acquisition of geographically qualified leads.

What sets me apart is my specialized focus on the two most critical aspects of modern search: helping local businesses appear exactly where their customers are searching (Geo-Targeting), and optimizing for the way people actually ask questions (Answer Engine Optimization). This dual expertise has enabled me to deliver exceptional ROI for service-based businesses including tree services, roofing companies, lawn care providers, and real estate agencies.
